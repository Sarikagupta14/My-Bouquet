About

The plumeria flower represents birth and love;
spring and new beginnings. It's an extremely positive
and hopeful symbol, so it's no surprise that it's
extremely popular and adored. In Hawaiian culture,
the plumeria can be used to symbolize a woman's romantic
status when worn in the hair.

Bloom Season

* Healthy and productive plumeria usually starts blooming
in early summer, which generally continues into early fall.
