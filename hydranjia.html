About

The hydrangea represents gratitude, grace and beauty.
It also radiates abundance because of the lavish number
of flowers and the generous round shape. Its colours
symbolise love, harmony and peace.


Bloom Season

*The hydrangea blooming season depends upon the type and
cultivar as well as your planting zone.
*Most new growth hydrangeas put on buds in
early summer to bloom in the following spring,
summer and early fall seasons.
