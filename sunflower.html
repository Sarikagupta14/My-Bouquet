About

Sunflowers symbolise loyalty, adoration thanks to the myth
of Clytie and Apollo. And, because of their association
with the sun, sunflowers are well-known for being a happy
flower and the perfect bloom for a summer flower delivery
to brighten someone's mood!

Bloom Season

*It takes about thirty days for a sunflower to bloom.
* The reproductive phase will begin in June and end in July or August.
