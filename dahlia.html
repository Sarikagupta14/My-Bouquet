About

Dahlias are also known as “Queen of the Garden” because
they grow well in poor soil and withstand drought conditions.
It is believed that Dhalia flowers bring happiness and
good fortune. They are also considered lucky
because they bloom during the month of Ramadan.

They're even the National Garden Bureau's flower of the year for 2019!

Bloom Season

* Dahlias are warm-weather flowers that bloom mid-summer through fall.
*They begin to bloom a couple of months after the soil warms, and
continue to bloom until early to mid-fall.
